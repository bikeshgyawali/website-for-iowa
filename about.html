Our History

A trusted name
on every road

NorthStar Gas was founded with a simple belief — that every driver deserves a reliable, clean, and welcoming place to stop. What started as a single station has grown into a regional network trusted by thousands of drivers every day.

We have always invested in our communities, our staff, and our facilities. Every NorthStar location is held to the same high standard of cleanliness, service, and value that our customers have come to expect over the years.

As the world changes, so do we — expanding our services to include EV charging, expanded convenience offerings, and delivery through DoorDash to meet drivers wherever they are.
